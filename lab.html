Case notes

Carpentry Companion public surface

Goal: make support and compliance routes clear without introducing a heavy platform.

Approach: static pages, direct links, shared templates, and simple update paths.

Result: durable public pages that satisfy app listing needs and remain easy to edit.

Main site redesign track

Goal: turn a dense personal dashboard into a clear studio front door.

Approach: stronger hierarchy, calmer spacing, fewer competing sections, more authored copy.

Result: cleaner pathways into work, notes, and products without losing personality.

Project + product syncing

Goal: reduce manual updates while keeping fallback behavior reliable.

Approach: generated JSON catalogs with static fallbacks and no framework overhead.

Result: listings stay fresh, and the site still degrades gracefully.
